Search Engineer

Type: Full time
Experience: 6+ years Description:

Wireless Grids is looking for an experienced, smart, fearless search engineer to join our engineering and design team. This position will be responsible for design, implementation, tuning, and management of a large scale search system for personal data.

Duties Include:

  • Designing and implementing a custom search engine for large amounts of personal data.
  • Develop operational processes for monitoring and maintenance. Will have to work closely with both Engineering and Operations teams going forward.
  • Combine off the shelf solutions with custom code.
  • Implement Proof-of-Concept and Demo implementations for weird requests that come from the business development team.
  • Provide daily and 24/7 on-call support

Requirements and Skills:

  • B.S. Computer Science, Mathematics, Engineering, or equivalent experience.
  • Expert experience with search programming and performance tuning.
  • Lucene, Sphinx, Xapian experience a plus.
  • Expert understanding of large scale schema-less data engines.
  • Operations experience in a high-volume, Linux environment.
  • Expert development of custom ETL in previous jobs.
  • Expert understanding of ETL techniques and best practices.
  • Experience handling large amounts of data.
  • Ruby and Java development experience required.
  • Erlang, C++ development experience a plus.
  • Knowledge of Hadoop Distributed FS, GlusterFS, or other distributed file systems.
  • Strong analytical skills including the ability to define problems, collect data, and articulate possible solutions.
  • Basic networking knowledge a must.
  • Self starter. You do not need micromanagement.
  • Good sense of humor.

Network Ops

Type: Full time
Experience: 4+ years Description:

We need a network/operations person to help manage the infrastructure for both our development group as well as future production deployments. Some of our software will be deployed to limited test groups, so we want to capture and analyze data from those trials. We've been getting along using a combination of Chef and Slicehost/EC2, but we need a dedicated person to take us to the next level of growth.

Requirements and Skills:

  • manage both development and production infrastructure
  • comfortable with cloud/virtual (amazon ec2, engine yard, rightscale, etc.)
  • configuration management (chef, puppet, etc.)
  • monitoring at network, server, and service levels
  • develop procedures for continuity, backup/recovery, security
  • create internal software when needed, use off the shelf when appropriate
  • Ruby and Java experience required
  • preferable to have experience with:
    • xmpp servers (ejabberd, openfire)
    • sql based DBs (mysql, postgres)
    • non-sql data stores (couchDB, mongo, etc.)
    • distributed file system (Hadoop FS, Gluster FS, or other)
