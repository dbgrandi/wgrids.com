About Us

At WGC we believe that the true potential of your personal digital infrastructure has yet to be realised. We aim to change that ...

Originally born out of a National Science Foundation academic research project, WGC has been undertaking research and development around the concepts of personal Grid networks for a number of years.

In 2009 the company was acquired by Varsity Media Group allowing us to complete the transition from academic curio to fully fledged commercial organisation.
